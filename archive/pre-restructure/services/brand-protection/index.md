---
title: "Secure a brand name for multiple TLDs"
---

A special service to protect your trademark is offered by several registries. Instead of defensive registrations of your brand name, you can secure your name through this service, prevent cybersquatting and reserve your name for future use. The trademark term is therefore locked from registration for all TLDs supported by that registry operator at a fraction of the cost of defensive registrations.

For AdultBlock, we offer many API commands to allow you to easily manage your blocking services. The registry is constantly updating their API functionality, which we will continuously work to integrate with. For functionalities not yet available, our support team will gladly assist you on these maters. Additional details can be found in the associated articles regarding AdultBlock.

For DPML (Donuts) the service will be manually registered for you by our support team, which will also inform you about the required data from your side. Additional details can be found in the associated articles regarding DPML(Donuts).

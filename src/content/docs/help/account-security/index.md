Our password policy for CentralNic Reseller accounts and users of CentralNic Reseller accounts requires passwords to match the following criteria:

-   length between 8 and 32 characters
-   contains numbers
-   contains upper and lower case letters
-   contains special characters (non-number or non-letter)

EPP: The EPP standard allows a maximum password length of 16 characters. Nonetheless, for our EPP gateway we increased the limit to 32 characters.

Special Characters: All UTF-8 characters are allowed.

Affected are all newly modified or created passwords for CentralNic Reseller accounts and users.

In general, we advise to change passwords on a regular basis. Furthermore, we advise to use additional features to secure your account, like [two-factor authentication](2-factor-authentication/) for the CentralNic control panel and [IP whitelisting](ip-whitelist/) for other interfaces.

## Newsletters

We continuously communicates via Newsletter about technical releases, promotions, price changes, maintenances and further news around our services.

## Maintenances

Regular maintenances are processed throughout the year. These maintenances are communicated through Newsletters and our Status Website.

## Regular OT&E bi-weekly Maintenance

Starting May 2nd, 2024 and ongoing afterwards throughout the year every two weeks on a Wednesday in even calendar weeks at 09:00 CEST / 07:00 UTC (during summer time), 09:00 CET / 08:00 UTC (during European winter time) we will have a maintenance on our OT&E with limited reachability.

The maintenance windows should not exceed 2 hours. The regular bi-weekly is not affecting any LIVE system and thus is not listed on our status page.

## Status Website

We inform via our status website by providing updates on the operational state of our system as well as announcing maintenances. You can find the status website at: [http://status.centralnicreseller.com](http://status.centralnicreseller.com)

## Subscription for Newsletters and Maintenances

You can manage your subscriptions for Newsletters and Maintenances inside your CentralNic Reseller account at "Settings" -> "Account Data" on the tabs "Newsletter" and "Maintenance".

To ensure you're receiving these mails properly, please make sure you have relevant email addresses inserted.

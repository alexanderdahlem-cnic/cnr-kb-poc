---
title: "New and renamed Events"
---

All events are used for all SSL certificate products, regardless of the used CA. As of March 13th 2018 we are going to rename and introduce the events for SSL certificates. This is necessary to be able to offer more detailed information about all actions around SSL certificates.

**CERTIFICATE\_REGISTRATION::REGISTRATION\_SUCCESSFUL**

Certificate registration was successful. Old value: CERTIFICATE::ADD\_SUCCESS

**CERTIFICATE\_REGISTRATION::REGISTRATION\_FAILED**

Certificate registration has failed.

**CERTIFICATE\_REGISTRATION::REGISTRATION\_NOTIFY**

Notification during certificate registration. Old value: N/A

**CERTIFICATE\_RENEWAL::RENEWAL\_SUCCESSFUL**

Certificate renewal was successful. Old value: CERTIFICATE::RENEW\_SUCCESS

**CERTIFICATE\_RENEWAL::RENEWAL\_FAILED**

Certificate renewal has failed. Old value: CERTIFICATE::RENEW\_FAILED

**CERTIFICATE\_RENEWAL::RENEWAL\_NOTIFY**

Notification during certificate renewal. Old value: N/A

**CERTIFICATE\_MODIFICATION::MODIFICATION\_SUCCESSFUL**

Certificate modification was successful. Old value: N/A

**CERTIFICATE\_MODIFICATION::MODIFICATION\_FAILED**

Certificate modification has failed. Old value: N/A

**CERTIFICATE\_MODIFICATION::MODIFICATION\_NOTIFY**

Notification during certificate modification. Old value: N/A

**CERTIFICATE\_REISSUE::REISSUE\_SUCCESSFUL**

Certificate reissue was successful. Old value: CERTIFICATE::REISSUE\_SUCCESS

**CERTIFICATE\_REISSUE::REISSUE\_FAILED**

Certificate reissue has failed. Old value: CERTIFICATE::REISSUE\_FAILED

**CERTIFICATE\_REISSUE::REISSUE\_NOTIFY**

Notification during certificate reissue. Old value: N/A

**CERTIFICATE\_DELETION::DELETION\_SUCCESSFUL**

Certificate deletion was successful. Old value: CERTIFICATE::DELETE\_SUCCESS

**CERTIFICATE\_DELETION::DELETION\_FAILED**

Certificate deletion has failed. Old value: CERTIFICATE::DELETE\_FAILED

**CERTIFICATE\_DELETION::DELETION\_NOTIFY**

Notification during certificate deletion. Old value: N/A

## Price adjustments for Sectigo MultiDomain and Unified Communications products

We would like to inform you about an upcoming pricing adjustment for SAN-capable Sectigo products MultiDomain DV, OV, EV and Unified Communications DV and OV Certificates.

For SAN-capable products (MultiDomain DV, OV, EV; UnifiedCommunication Certificate DV, OV), a minimum of 3 domains is charged per order by default. **Effective Tuesday December 10, 2024 00:00 UTC**, this minimum cost will be factored into our base pricing. This means that the base price covers 3 domains, or 2 if a wildcard domain is included, with additional single or wildcard domains charged separately.

The adjustment is due to a misinterpretation of Sectigo’s pricing structure at the initial setup, which led us to offer SANs at no additional cost. While we are committed to providing quality products at competitive prices, we must revise our pricing to avoid selling below cost.

  

## The following SSL price types will be adjusted and include 3 domains

  

### New SSL 2.0 API

**MultiDomainDV**

-   multidomaindv-base: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   multidomaindv-wildcardbase: pricing includes 1 wildcard domain and 2 single domains

**MultiDomainOV**

-   multidomainov-base: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   multidomainov-wildcardbase: pricing includes 1 wildcard domain and 2 single domains

**MultiDomainEV**

-   multidomainev-base: pricing includes 3 single domains

**UnifiedCommunicationsDV**

-   unifiedcommunicationsdv-base: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   unifiedcommunicationsdv-wildcardbase: pricing includes 1 wildcard domain and 2 single domains

**UnifiedCommunicationsOV**

-   unifiedcommunicationsov-base: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   unifiedcommunicationsov-wildcardbase: pricing includes 1 wildcard domain and 2 single domains

Pricing for single domain and wildcard domain SANs will not change.

  

### Old SSL API

**UnifiedCommunicationsDV**

-   UnifiedCommunicationsDV: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   UnifiedCommunicationsDV-1san: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   UnifiedCommunicationsDV-2san: pricing includes 3 single domains

**UnifiedCommunicationsOV**

-   UnifiedCommunicationsOV: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   UnifiedCommunicationsOV-1san: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   UnifiedCommunicationsOV-2san: pricing includes 3 single domains

**MultiDomainEV**

-   MultiDomainEV: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   MultiDomainEV-1san: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   MultiDomainEV-2san: pricing includes 3 single domains

**MultiDomainOV**

-   MultiDomainOV: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   MultiDomainOV-1san: pricing includes 3 single domains
-   MultiDomainOV-2san: pricing includes 3 single domains

  

For configurations with more than 3 domains (i.e., 3 to 24 SANs), pricing is calculated by incrementally adding the cost of a single domain per additional SAN.

Wildcard domains are not supported in our old SSL API.

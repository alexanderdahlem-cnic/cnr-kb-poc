Whether for your own or your clients' domain names, DNS stability and reliability is crucial for success in the domain (DNS) hosting area. Take advantage of our long-time experience in DNS management and monitoring and use our powerful KeyDNS services. Furthermore, DNS zone management for external domains is integrated into our CentralNic Reseller reseller model. Consequently, the hosted DNS service for external domains may be provided to subresellers and invoiced accordingly.

KeyDNS is designed to serve as your solution for DNS zone file management and also integrates as Slave NS setup for your Master NS.

---
title: "How to restore a cPanel useraccount?"
---

The account can only be restored for 30 days.

<div class="api-io api-io--command"><span class="api-io__label">Command</span>
<pre class="api-io__code"><code>COMMAND = RestoreHosting
CLASS = cpanel
TYPE = useraccount
ACCOUNT = cPanelAccountIDofEndcustomer</code></pre></div>
